WIMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Summary: WIMS (Where Is My Shit) is a privacy-first extension that keeps all your data local. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your computer.

What WIMS Does

WIMS Capture is a Chrome extension that helps you search through your AI chat conversations. It works with:

  • ChatGPT (chatgpt.com)
  • Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)
  • Perplexity AI (perplexity.ai)

The extension captures conversation content from these sites and sends it to your local WIMS server for indexing and search.

Data Collection

WIMS does not collect any personal data.

The extension accesses:

  • AI chat conversation content on supported platforms (chatgpt.com, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai)
  • Page titles and URLs of conversations
  • Timestamps of when conversations were accessed

This data is never transmitted to external servers. It is only sent to your local WIMS server running on your own computer (localhost or 127.0.0.1).

Data Storage

All data captured by WIMS is stored locally:

  • Browser storage: Extension settings (like your WIMS server URL and API key) are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device
  • Local WIMS server: Conversation content is stored in a local database on your computer, managed by the WIMS server application

No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

Third-Party Services

WIMS does not use any third-party services.

The extension only communicates with:

  • Your local WIMS server (localhost or 127.0.0.1)
  • The AI chat platforms you visit (chatgpt.com, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai) - only to read conversation content from the page

Permissions

WIMS requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your extension settings (server URL and API key) locally
  • alarms: To periodically sync conversations from supported platforms
  • host_permissions (localhost): To communicate with your local WIMS server

WIMS does not request access to browse your entire browsing history or access other websites beyond the specific AI chat platforms.

User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Stop capturing: Disable or uninstall the extension at any time through Chrome's extension settings
  • Delete stored data: Delete conversation data through the WIMS server interface or by removing the WIMS database files on your computer
  • Clear extension settings: Clear extension settings by uninstalling the extension or clearing Chrome's local storage

Data Security

Since all data stays on your local computer:

  • You control physical and network access to your data
  • WIMS server uses API key authentication to protect access to your local data
  • No data is exposed to external networks or cloud services
